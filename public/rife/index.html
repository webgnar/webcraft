Royal Raymond Rife

Devloped a opitcal microscope capable of viewing organisms 25,000X.

Here is a timeline of Royal Rife's life.

  • 1888 - Born
  • 1913 - Awarded an honarary PhD from the University of Heidelsberg
  • 1913-1920 - Worked for the U.S. government and for Zeiss in Germany. Developed an optical tool for Harry Timken to monitor ball-bearing production.
  • 1920's - Timken provides funding for a research lab in San Diego to study effects of electromagnetic phenomena on disease-causing agents; Rife develops the Universal Microscope and the Beam Ray Machine.
  • 1931 - Rife's work endorsed by noted microbiologist Arthur Kendall (Northwestern University) and physician Millbank Johnson (AMA West Coast); banquet proclaiming "The End to All Diseases" held.
  • 1934 - "Clinical" trials of the Beam Ray Machine; 16 patients "cured" of "cancers."
  • 1938 - "Dread Disease Germs Destroyed By Rays..."; Morris Fishbeid and the AMA take notice
  • 1940 - All support for Rife and the technology disappears
