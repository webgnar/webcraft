Who am I

I am a visual artist from Chicago. I began making art at the age of 17 and primarily focus on visual art, specifically, painting. I am visually influenced from video games and have been exploring the art of game design, animation, and computer coding. I am a conscientious hard worker, and enjoy pushing myself to learn new things. I am also working on web development and contributing to a website called Skatehive. When I work, I channel my higher self to immerse myself in the moment and nourish the flow state.
